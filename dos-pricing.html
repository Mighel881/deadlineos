Still have questions?

What happens when I reach the Free limit?
+
After 5 AI analyses per day a gentle notice appears. All other features — Tasks, Timer, Gamification, Mood Tracker — remain fully available. No hard block, no forced upgrade dialog.
Is there a free trial for Pro/Team?
+
Yes — 14 days full Pro for free, no credit card required. For Team there is a 30-day trial for companies on request. Just write to us.
How does the money-back guarantee work?
+
Not satisfied in the first 14 days? Just send an email — no questions, full refund. No complicated process.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?
+
Yes, anytime. Upgrade becomes active immediately and is charged proportionally. Downgrade runs until the end of the current billing period. Your data remains fully preserved.
What happens to my data when I cancel?
+
All data is stored in the browser (LocalStorage) — we have no access to it. When you cancel you only lose the premium features, not your tasks or notes.
Are there discounts for students or NGOs?
+
Yes — 50% for verified students and non-profit organizations. Send us an email with proof. Special conditions apply for schools and universities.
